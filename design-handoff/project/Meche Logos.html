03 — Notes

The accent grave is doing a lot of work here — it reads as a single highlight, a strand of hair, a wink. The coral accent (oklch ~0.66 0.13 38) is warm and salon-friendly without being girly; works against cream, ink, and on tile. Two systems to pick between: editorial (Fraunces + Instrument Serif) for a quiet-luxury beauty feel, or tech-forward (Geist + Geist Mono) for an Arc/Linear-tier modern app brand. Say which direction lands and I'll refine to a single mark + full system (icon, color, type, voice).
