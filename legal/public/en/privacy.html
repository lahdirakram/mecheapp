Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Mèche is an AI hairstyle try-on service. This policy explains what data we process and why.

Data controller

The data controller is AML Technologies, a sole proprietorship operated by Mohamed LAHDIR, 12 Chemin de la Redoute, 94800 Villejuif, France (SIREN 883 909 186). Contact: support@mecheapp.com.

Data we process

  • Photos — the selfies you take or images you import, and the images generated from them, to produce your hairstyle previews.
  • Email and account identifiers — to create your account and sign you in (including via Apple or Google).
  • Purchase data — when you buy credits, the transaction is handled by Apple's App Store or Google Play. We never receive or store your payment card details.
  • Technical data — a device notification token (if you enable notifications) and limited logs needed for security and operation.

Legal basis

  • Account creation and generating your previews: performance of the contract.
  • Authentication, abuse prevention, security: legitimate interest.
  • Notifications: your consent (revocable anytime in the app).
  • Retention of billing data: legal obligation.

Use

Your photos are used only to generate your hairstyle previews. They are never made public or sold, and are not used to identify you (no facial recognition).

Processors and sharing

We do not sell your data. It is shared only with the technical providers strictly necessary to run the service:

  • Supabase — database hosting and image storage.
  • Google (Gemini) — image generation: your photo is sent to Google's AI model for the sole purpose of producing the preview.
  • Apple App Store / Google Play — processing credit purchases.
  • Expo — sending push notifications.

Transfers outside the EU

Some providers (including Google) are located outside the European Economic Area, notably in the United States. Such transfers are covered by the appropriate safeguards required by the GDPR (an adequacy decision or standard contractual clauses).

Retention and deletion

Your photos and previews are kept while your account is active. You can delete your tries at any time, and permanently delete your account and all your data from within the app (Profile → Account → Delete my account), or by contacting us. Billing data is kept for the legally required period.

Security

Data is encrypted in transit and stored privately. Generated images, which contain your face, are accessible only to you.

Minors

The service is not intended for people under 15. We do not knowingly collect data from minors below that age.

Your rights

Under the GDPR, you have rights of access, rectification, erasure, restriction, objection and portability. To exercise them, contact support@mecheapp.com. You may also lodge a complaint with the competent supervisory authority, notably the CNIL in France (cnil.fr).
