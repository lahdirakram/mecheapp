Account deletion

Delete your account

You can permanently delete your Mèche account and all your data, either from within the app or by contacting us.

From the app

  1. Open the Mèche app and sign in.
  2. Go to the Profile tab.
  3. Open the Account section, then tap Delete my account.
  4. Confirm with Delete permanently.

Deletion is immediate and cannot be undone.

If you no longer have access to the app

Email support@mecheapp.com from your account's email address. We process the request within 30 days.

Data that is deleted

  • Your profile and account information
  • Your photos (selfies and generated images)
  • Your AI tries and generations
  • Your saved looks
  • Your credits
  • Your devices registered for notifications

This data is permanently erased. Billing data is kept for the legally required period, then deleted.
