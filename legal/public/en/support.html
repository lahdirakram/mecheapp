Support

Need a hand?

A question, a result that looks off, a billing or account issue. Email us and we'll get back fast.

Contact us

The easiest way is email. Write to support@mecheapp.com and describe the problem. To speed things up, include your phone model and a screenshot if you can.

Response time

We reply as fast as we can, in English and French.

Manage or delete your account

You can permanently delete your account and all your data directly in the app: Profile → Account → Delete my account. You can also email support@mecheapp.com and we'll take care of it.
